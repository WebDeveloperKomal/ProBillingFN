Our Other Features

Manage finances and inventory efficiently from a single platform for better control and organization.

Prices

Set purchase and sales price for every product in your inventory.


Quantity

For your product stock, choose between different quantity types.


Stock Keeping Units

Give your products unique Stock Keeping Units (SKUs) to simplify inventory tracking.

Team Access

Establish a separate account for bookkeepers or accountants to manage your finances effectively.


Permissions Management

Manage visibility by inviting users, setting permissions, and ensuring data privacy when necessary. Control access effectively.

Budget Planner

Enhance your business finances management through integrated budget planning for greater control and efficiency.

Multiple Currencies

Create invoices and view reports in your preferred currency


Bank Accounts

Easily add bank accounts to quickly check your financial situation with just a few clicks.

Product Tax

Create tax rates for products to ensure automatic application during transactions.

Categories

Create different product categories to enhance stock management and improve reporting accuracy.
